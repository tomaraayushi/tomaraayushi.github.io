About my skincare obsession.

To get the most benefit out of a daily skincare regimen, no matter what your skin type, you should start with three simple steps:

  • Cleanse
  • Treat
  • Moisturize

Well,treating means incorporating serums or creams packed full of beneficial skincare ingredients like vitamin C, retinol, alpha hydroxy acids and others.

I like Vitamin C and retinol the most because these two are the best anti-aging ingredients.
